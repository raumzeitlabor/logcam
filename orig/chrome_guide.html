Step 1: Download the Wi-Fi SD downloader

Step 2: Click the Chrome menu on the browser toolbar, select Tools, and select Extensions
or Open a new tab and type chrome://extensions/ in the address bar and press Enter to open the Chrome extension page.


Step 3: Drag and Drop the file "wifisd.crx" that was downloaded in step 1 to the Chrome extension page.


Step 4: The Wi-Fi SD downloader will be installed automatically.


Step 5: Switch to Wi-Fi SD photo page and click on the Wi-Fi SD downloader's icon.
